About #You Never Walk Alone

This is where you will find all of my story-worthy travel moments from the last five years. Traveling from Nanjing, China, to countries in Europe and South America, I enjoy visiting new places to learn new languages, learn about new cultures, try new foods, and make new friends. Whether you will be traveling with family, friends, or just yourself, remember that you are never alone because you have guidance and will always be meeting new people and things along the way. Come and visit the website if you do not know where to go next!
