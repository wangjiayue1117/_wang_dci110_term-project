Top Destinations

1. Salzburg, Austria
2. Florence, Italy
3. Budapest, Hungary
4. Rome, Italy

Salzburg, Austria

World Heritage City, Mozart's bithplace, the meeting place for top culture at the annual Salzburg Festival, and interspersed between are spacious squares, hidden inner courtyards, small alleyways and two mountains in the middle of the city. To describe Salzburg with just one word is impossible.

Top Things to Do in Salzburg:
♦ Go on a 3-hour bike tour to cycle through the city of Sound of Music & Mozart
♦ Climb to Fortress Hohensalzburg after 7 p.m. (because it's free after 7!)
♦ Walk down Getreidegasse to find local traditional businesses and souvenir shops

Florence, Italy

Located in the Tuscany region of Italy, Florence is a captivating Italy destination that Mark Twain once described as a “city of dreams.” With its abundance of timeless art, rustic Tuscan cuisine, incredible boutique shopping, and mesmerizing views at every turn, the city of Florence is nothing short of spectacular.

Top Things to Do in Florence:
♦ Climb the Dome of the Duomo or the Bell Tower for a panoramic view of the city
♦ Discover the best of Florence's street art (this is a MUST!)
♦ Pose with Michelangelo’s David at the Accademia Gallery

Budapest, Hungary

Budapest has something for everyone – from dramatic history and flamboyant architecture to healing thermal waters and a nightlife that is unrivalled in Eastern and Central Europe. The city definitely lives up to its stellar reputation.

Top Things to Do in Budapest:
♦ Cruise down the Danube right before the sun sets (timing is the key!)
♦ Climb to the top of Fisherman’s Bastion and enjoy a chimney cone with ice cream
♦ Score a good deal at Central Market Hall

Rome, Italy

A heady mix of haunting ruins, awe-inspiring art and vibrant street life, Italy’s hot-blooded capital, Rome, is one of the world’s most romantic and charismatic cities.

Top Things to Do in Rome:
♦ Make a wish in front of the Trevi Fountain (what if your dream comes true)
♦ Take A Roman Holiday Tour Of Rome
♦ Spend a day in Vatican Museums (there are so much treasure to see)

Vienna, Austria

Baroque streetscapes and imperial palaces set the stage for Vienna's artistic and musical masterpieces alongside its coffee-house, wine-tavern culture and vibrant epicurean and design scenes.

Top Things to Do in Vienna:
♦ Try diverse cakes, pastries, and desserts in different coffee houses
♦ Visit the majestic Hofburg Imperial Palace to learn about Austrians' beloved Emperor Franz and Empress Sisi
♦ See a world-class performance at Wiener Staatsoper and Musikverein
